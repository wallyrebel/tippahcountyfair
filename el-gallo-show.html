★ Rules ★

  • All poultry must weigh between 4–6 lbs., except for miniature breeds.
  • Everything should be trimmed with the exception of young stags, orientals, and hens.
  • Each exhibitor is responsible for bringing their own drop pens.
  • All poultry must be from an NPIP-certified flock or have a health record from a veterinarian.
  • No aggressive birds will be allowed. Judges' decisions are final.
