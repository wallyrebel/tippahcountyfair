From the President

Welcome to Your Fair

"The Tippah County Fair is more than an event. It is a tradition."

For 81 years, our community has gathered each summer at the Tippah County Fairgrounds to celebrate local talent, hard work, and tradition. As one of the longest-standing and largest county fairs in Mississippi, the Tippah County Fair remains a beloved highlight of the season.

This year we've worked hard to bring the fairgrounds to life for eight full days of family fun and entertainment — from championship-worthy livestock shows to a legendary rodeo. So come join the fun and be a part of the legacy. Let's enjoy an unforgettable week at the 81st Tippah County Fair!

— Tim Needham, President · Tippah County Fair Board

🏅 Premier Exhibitor

A cash award presented to one Cloverbud, one Junior, and one Senior 4-Her (ages 8–18 by January 1, 2026). Winners are chosen on a point system for placings and ribbons earned on any exhibit, plus participation and community service for the Fair.

Participants must earn points in three divisions. Chairman: Robert Farrow.

🍢 Concession Stand Row

Youth organizations, churches, and non-profits are welcome to be a part of Concession Stand Row. Food items only may be sold, and spaces are limited.

Contact Robert Farrow at (662) 837-1907 for availability or questions.

🎪 Carload Night

Friday, August 7, 2026 — bring the whole crew! $8 per person or $25 per carload or $40 per vanload. Everyone must be in seatbelts.
